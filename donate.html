Why Your Support Matters

Every donation, no matter the size, helps us take one step closer to a sustainable future. Your funds are used for:

  • Purchasing saplings for reforestation ($10 plants 5 trees)
  • Maintaining solar equipment in rural schools
  • Funding research into local climate solutions
  • Providing tools and safety gear for our volunteers

Donation FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear most from our supporters.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Bright-World Foundation is a registered non-profit in Ghana. Donors requesting a receipt for tax purposes can request one via our Contact page after completing a donation.

Can I set up a recurring monthly donation?

Yes. Choose your preferred amount above and select the "recurring" option with your payment provider, or mention it in the message field and our team will follow up to set it up.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit and debit cards, Mobile Money, and PayPal, so you can give in the way that's most convenient for you.

How is my donation used?

Funds go directly toward reforestation, renewable energy installation, water projects, and volunteer support, as outlined in the "Why Your Support Matters" section above.
