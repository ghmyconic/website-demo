Our Impact Projects

We are actively working on various fronts to protect and restore our environment.

Project 1: Community Development

Location: Ashanti Region

Status: Ongoing

We are planting native tree species to restore degraded forest reserves and create carbon sinks.

Impact: 150,000 trees planted to date.

Project 2: Green and Renewable Energy

Location: Northern Region

Status: Active

Providing solar-powered lighting systems to off-grid rural schools and clinics.

Impact: 45 schools now have reliable electricity.

Project 3: Coastal Cleanup

Location: Western Region Coastline

Status: Monthly Recurring

Organizing community-led cleanups to remove plastic waste from our beautiful beaches.

Impact: Over 20 tons of plastic removed annually.

Project 4: Eco-Schools Program

Location: Nationwide

Status: Expanding

Integrating environmental education into the school curriculum through interactive workshops.

Impact: 120 schools participating in the program.

Project 5: Pure Water Wells

Location: Volta Region

Status: Active

Constructing sustainable solar-powered wells to provide clean drinking water to communities.

Impact: 15,000 people now have access to safe water.

Project 6: Green Rooftops

Location: Accra & Kumasi

Status: Pilot Phase

Promoting urban agriculture through rooftop gardening to improve food security and reduce heat.

Impact: 25 rooftop gardens established in city centers.
